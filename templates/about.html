ABOUT US

WHAT IS HESS ABOUT?

HESS stands for Humane Education Sustainable Solutions

Its a platform for people to incorporate humane education and prosocial learning in institutions and at workplaces. Humane/ Prosocial education enables learners to develop skills such as critical thinking, empathy, problem solving and building perspective and enabling them to model those values that are the basis for successful citizens.

Our tailored services are desinged for educators and other professionals who would like to develop best practices in their respective areas to promote a culture of empathy and compassion.
